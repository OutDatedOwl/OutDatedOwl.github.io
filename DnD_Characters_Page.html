CURRENT CHARACTERS

EVALIA SERENGLOW
Evalia Serenglow is a female High Elf eldritch knight from the city of Neverwinter. Her parents were members of the Lords' Alliance, and she always wanted to follow in their footsteps. She was personally trained by Sildar Hallwinter, and joined the Lords' Alliance after completing her training. After taking quick action in leading her allies to fight a White Dragon, Evalia was promoted Redknife of the Lords' Alliance, and is now the leader of her own team, "The Fab Five". Her nickname is the "Ice Queen", due to her ice based magic and at times cold demeanor.
URZOTH KETH
Urzoth Keth is a male Half-Orc rogue, from the Many-Arrows Orc tribe. After fighting in the War of the Silver Marches, Keth grew sick of his fathers barbaric nature, and decided to leave his tribe. He met a woman named Sasha in a small village called Floodblest, and the two fell in love. One day the village was raided by Drow and Duergar slavers, and Keth turned to the Lords' Alliance in Neverwinter for help. Many lives were saved during the "Ketht", and ever since then, Keth decided to join the Lords' Alliance, to help others as they helped him.
OCTAVIO THE JUST
Octavio The Just is a male Human evocation wizard from Flea Bottom Alley, the roughest and toughest alley in the city of Neverwinter. Octavio grew up as a street orphan, stealing to get by. One day he stole a magic spell book, and was able to learn the spell Mage Hand from it. After being caught using his magic to steal from a Neverwinter Guard, the guard suggested he put his talent to use for good, rather than rot in prison. Octavio went to the Neverwinter Mage Academy, and eventually joined the Lords' Alliance after finding a new direction in life
DANTE ROADS
Dante Roads is a male Aasimar monk. He was rescued from a bandit raid as an infant, but his parents did not survive. Dante was raised in the Crossroad Keep orphanage in Neverwinter, and always wanted to become a member of the Lords' Alliance just like the man who saved him. After joining for a short while, he was noticed by a man named Hlam, who saw something special in him and decided to train him. After a few years of training under Hlam, Dante became incredibly skilled in martial arts fighting, and rejoined the Lords' Alliance to continue pursuing his goal.
