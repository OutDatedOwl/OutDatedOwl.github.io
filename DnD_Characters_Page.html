CURRENT CHARACTERS

ORM WYRMEND
Orm Wyrmend is a male human fighter who comes from a once famous clan of dragon slayers. He is one of the few survivors of the destruction of his home, Thundertree. He has a high opinion of himself due to his bloodline, but he is still young and has much to learn. He adventures to become stronger, and to be worthy enough to one day reclaim his heritage as a dragon slayer.
KUMORI HARADA
Kumori Harada is a male Tiefling rogue, and was an orphan raised by the church in Phandalin. Not much is known about Kumori's past, and he keeps details about his personal life close to the chest. While initially cold, he seems to be warming up to the party. His "sudden need for work" is the driving force behind his current adventuring life.
TALIESIN BERSK
Taliesin Bersk is a male Tiefling bard. He was passing through Phandalin, playing music, courting women, when he came upon Tharralac, Nim and Kumori while they made their way to the Miner's Exchange. Taliesin, being a wandering bard is always keen on adventure. When asked if he would like to join the group, he quickly accepted.
OTHELLO CARMELO RODRIGUEZ ROMERO RIVERA GARCIA GONZALEZ GUTIERREZ TORRES III ESQUIRE
Othello is a male Human fighter. He was born in Helm's Hold. An Orphan, raised by the Church of Helm to become one of their Everwatch Knights. Ironclad knights hired out by groups all across Faerun to act as bodyguards in order to generate revenue. From an early age, the Church had instilled a duty to protect the innocent at all costs in their knightly order. Helm's word was law. Othello was raised to be a devote follower of the Church of Helm. A large golden cross hangs off Othello's neck to demonstrate his faith.
AYLA LIGHTBRINGER
Ayla is a female Elf Paladin. She worhips the Goddess Chauntea of life and bounty. Ayla is from Silverymoon, an Elven city far from Neverwinter and the Sword Coast. A survivor of an Ice Dragon attack on her hometown, Ayla set out to assist anyone who had suffered the same fate as she. Wandering around the Sword Coast, spreading the word of Chauntea. Ayla comes across an Ice Dragon once again but this time with allies. She swiftly joined up with fellow adventurers to bring that Ice Dragon down.
