CURRENT CHARACTERS

THARRALAC "BIG HAT" IRONCHIN
Tharralac Ironchin is a male dwarven mage from Mirabar. Disregarding his clan's tradition to become a blacksmith, Tharralac decided to study at the Neverwinter Mage Academy. Overly confident, Tharralac casted "fireball", a spell far beyond his skill level thus destroying a section of the academy, his close friend, and permenantly scarring his face. After being expelled from the academy and with empty pockets. Tharralac decided to take on a life of adventure.
ORM WYRMEND
Orm Wyrmend is a male human fighter who comes from a once famous clan of dragon slayers. He is one of the few survivors of the destruction of his home, Thundertree. He has a high opinion of himself due to his bloodline, but he is still young and has much to learn. He adventures to become stronger, and to be worthy enough to one day reclaim his heritage as a dragon slayer.
WARRICK "NIM" FIDDLETRICK
Warrick "Nim" Fiddletrick is a male gnome cleric of Leira, the goddess of illusions and lies. He's lived a long life perfecting his illusion magic to conceal his secret village hidden within the Neverwinter Woods. He travels with his squirrel friend Alfredo, in order to learn magic strong enough to protect his people from all incoming threats.
KUMORI HARADA
Kumori Harada is a male human rogue, and was an orphan raised by the church in Phandalin. Not much is known about Kumori's past, and he keeps details about his personal life close to the chest. While initially cold, he seems to be warming up to the party. His "sudden need for work" is the driving force behind his current adventuring life.
