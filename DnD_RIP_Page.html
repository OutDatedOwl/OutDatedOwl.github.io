GRUBBLES
Tharralac Ironchin is a male dwarven mage from Mirabar. Disregarding his clan's tradition to become a blacksmith, Tharralac decided to study at the Neverwinter Mage Academy. Overly confident, Tharralac casted "fireball", a spell far beyond his skill level thus destroying a section of the academy, his close friend, and permenantly scarring his face. After being expelled from the academy and with empty pockets. Tharralac decided to take on a life of adventure.
