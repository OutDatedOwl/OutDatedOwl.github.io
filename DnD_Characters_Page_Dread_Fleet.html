FRONTO NIMBLEFOOT

Fronto Nimblefoot was born in Athkatla to a traveling group of merchants and circus performers. They worhsipped Waukeen and traveled all over Amn, selling their wares and entertaining guests. His father was a strongman and his mother an acrobat. At age 2, Fronto's father Bronto caught the boy curling his 100 lb dumbbells. Immediately Bronto knew Fronto was destined to be the circus's next Strong-man. He would grow up lifting during the day and tending to the circus animals at night.

One night, his mother Kronto walked in on Fronto telling jokes to the lion den. Fronto was blessed with the ability to speak with animals. With his unique gifts, business boomed. Until a spike in crime plagued the roads of Amn. Highwaymen and Orcs lurked about. Travel was dangerous. Business waned. As the circus bled money, Fronto suggested a solution. He become and adventurer to help pay for the circus costs. With word of finding work at The Yawning Portal, far from home in Waterdeep, Fronto quickly made his way North.
